DCS 2025 Events

Model United Nations

Experience diplomacy at its finest in our Model United Nations simulation. Delegates will represent different countries, debate global issues, and work towards meaningful resolutions in an environment that mirrors the actual United Nations.

Parliamentary Debate

Engage in high-level parliamentary style debates on pressing international issues. Develop your public speaking, critical thinking, and argumentation skills while competing against talented debaters from around the world.

Moot Court

Step into the shoes of international lawyers in our prestigious Moot Court competition. Present compelling arguments, analyze complex legal scenarios, and experience the thrill of international law in action.

Scavenger Hunt

Embark on an exciting diplomatic adventure! Our international scavenger hunt combines problem-solving, teamwork, and cultural knowledge in a race against time across various challenging scenarios.

Hackathon

Innovation meets diplomacy in our unique hackathon event. Work with fellow tech enthusiasts to develop solutions for global challenges, combining technology with international relations.

Socials

Network and build lasting connections at our exclusive social events. From formal galas to cultural nights, experience the lighter side of diplomacy while forming meaningful international friendships.

International Press

Be the voice of the summit as part of our International Press Corps. Cover events, conduct interviews, and create compelling content while learning about journalism in the context of international relations.
