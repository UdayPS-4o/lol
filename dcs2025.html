DCS 2025 Events

Model United Nations

Experience the thrill of debates on pressing issues worldwide with our model United Nations. Whether you want to contribute to world peace or talk about women's rights we’ve got you! .

Parliamentary Debate

Engage in heated discussions in our simulation of the Indian parliament or Commonly known as Indian committes. from bill reform to national security, participate in high-level debates with other members of parliament, to come to a consensus.

International Press

Be the voice of truth with the international press! Whether you want to capture the days with photos, words, or caricatures, DCS offers you an array of options to serve as a bridge between global events and public awareness!

Socials

An occasion to relax, interact, and build enduring relationships for the delegates. We, here at DCS have 2 trailblazing socials planned which are sure to add to the memories you take home!

Moot Court

Learn how court proceedings work as if you are in a real court with our moot court! designed to help participants understand how legal systems work, improve their research, public speaking, and argumentation skills, participate in DCS’s moot court for an unforgettable experience!

Scavenger Hunt

Connect the dots, solve clues, and test your teamwork skills with our scavenger hunt, Loot-era! The rush of searching for hidden objects or solving tricky riddles while racing against time keeps the excitement alive. Every clue brings a new challenge!
